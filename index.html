Homework 7: Nation's Imports and Exports in 2018

Caleb Tyler

This map uses Import and Export data in millions of dollars to display countries levels of trade surplus and trade deficit. This is done with the simple formula of Exports - Imports. The value of this formula is shown as a chloropleth for the countries with available data. Hovering over the nation will show its available data.
